Aiming to create a new academic field called “juris-informatics” and build a global research center on juris-informatics

　◆Based on two disciplines; "Law supported by AI" and "Law control of AI", the Center for Juris-Informatics is aiming at creating a new academic field called "juris-informatics" and building a global research center on juris-informatics by production of databases related with juris-informatics and investigation of usage of such databases.
