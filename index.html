Introducing Juris-Informatics: Pioneering a New Academic Field and Establishing a Global Research Center

At the forefront of artificial intelligence (AI) and legal research, the Center for Juris-Informatics aims to create a new academic field—"juris-informatics"—by integrating the areas of "Law supported by AI" and "Law control of AI." Our goal is to build a global research center focused on juris-informatics, through the development of relevant databases and the exploration of their applications.
